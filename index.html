Shelter to Soldier

Designing a responsive web based solution for both donors and applicants

Shelter to Soldier is a non-profit located in Oceanside, California. Their moto:” SAVING LIVES, TWO AT A TIME™” is the perfect summary of what they do as they bring post-9/11 combat veterans together with trained rescue dogs and as in their words they “help both recover and move forward.” We chose this non-profit because we loved how they were helping not only the animals but also our veterans at the same time.



Prototype



We defined two separate user personas: one being the donor and one being the veteran applicant.


Knowing that the Veteran might have potentially significant challenges due to their injuries we hypothesized they might need a clear and straight forward application process.


The donor would need information on why it is beneficial to donate and so we moved the statistics information which contained potentially sensitive information to the Veterans to the donate page.

We believe that by streamlining the application process, veterans with accessibility challenges will achieve greater application success while reducing abandoned applications. In the future Shelter to Soldier could track their abandoned application rate to see if our changes made a difference.
